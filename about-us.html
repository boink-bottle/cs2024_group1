About Us

In 2020, our founder knocked over their boring old water bottle, looked at it,
and set out on a mission to make hydration fun!

Our Mission

At BOINK!, we are a team of motivated individuals driven by a passion to make hydration a vibe. We wanted something that could live on your desk, star in your selfies, and make drinking water something to actually look forward to. Life's too short for boring bottles.
