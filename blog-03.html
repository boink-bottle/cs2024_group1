Sip in Style: 5 Ways to Style Your BOINK! Bottle 🥤

📅 October 5, 2023
👤 By BOINK! Style Team

Who Said Hydration Can't Be Fashionable?

As you drink with your BOINK! Bottle, you can make a bold fashion statement too! Whether you're headed to class, the gym, or just chilling at the café, your BOINK! bottle can be the perfect accessory to complete your look. Here are 5 ways to style your BOINK! Bottle.

LOOK 1: The Basic Singaporean Starter Pack 🇸🇬

For those quick trips to your favorite hawker centre, pair your bottle with something comfy. A basic tee (think Uniqlo), bag (think The Paper Bunny), and a claw clip. Bring your BOINK! bottle along to finish the look! Thank us later, trendstarter!

LOOK 2: The Active Life 💪

Ready to hit the gym? Pair your BOINK! bottle with your sporty outfit: comfy shorts, a gym tank top, and a cozy hoodie. Bonus points if your bottle matches your outfit. This look says you're ready to sweat it out, but you're also low-key a trendsetter.

LOOK 3: Professional Chic 💼

Who says you can't bring a little fun to the office? Pair your BOINK! bottle with a sleek blazer, tailored pants, and chic flats for a polished yet playful look. Whether you're meeting a client or just working from your favorite café, your bottle will add that extra touch of personality to your professional outfit.

LOOK 4: Lazy Sundays 🛋️

It's the weekend, and you're all about comfort without sacrificing style. Pair your BOINK! bottle with an oversized graphic tee, biker shorts, and chunky sneakers. Add a sling bag, and you're good to go – whether you're simply running errands or meeting friends for brunch.

LOOK 5: Finals Season 📝

For those long nights of study sessions and last-minute cramming before finals season, you'll need a look that's both comfortable and functional. Pair your bottle with a cozy hoodie, FBT shorts, and slippers. You'll be ready to power through the night with comfort and hydration.
